# Cold Outreach Best Practices 2026

## The Golden Rules

1. **Research before you write** — 30 seconds on their LinkedIn = 3x reply rate
2. **Short wins** — under 80 words for cold emails
3. **One CTA per email** — never give 3 options
4. **Lead with their problem, not your solution**
5. **Never attach files in Email 1** — triggers spam

## Subject Lines

✅ Works:
- `{{company}} network setup — quick question`
- `How [Competitor] handled [problem]`
- `Free [audit] for {{company}}`

❌ Avoid:
- `Following up` (everyone uses it)
- `RE: RE: RE:` (fake thread tricks are dead)
- `Hi {{first_name}}, just checking in`
- `Touching base`

## Send Times (IST)

**Best:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9:30–11am or 4:30–6pm  
**Avoid:** Monday morning, Friday afternoon, weekends

## Reply Rate Benchmarks (India B2B, 2026)

| Channel | Good | Excellent |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Cold email | 5%+ | 10%+ |
| LinkedIn connection | 30%+ | 50%+ |
| LinkedIn InMail | 8%+ | 15%+ |
| WhatsApp | 40%+ | 70%+ |

## Legal Basics

- **India:** No specific anti-spam law for B2B. Be respectful.
- **GDPR (EU):** Need legitimate interest basis. Include opt-out.
- **CAN-SPAM (US):** Include physical address + unsubscribe option.
- **Always:** Honour opt-outs immediately. Keep a suppression list.

## Deliverability Checklist

- [ ] Use a custom domain email (not Gmail/Hotmail)
- [ ] Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC records
- [ ] Warm up new email domains for 4 weeks before bulk sending
- [ ] Keep send volume under 50/day per inbox during warmup
- [ ] Use tools: Mailreach, Warmbox, or Instantly warmup

## Personalisation at Scale

**Tier 1 (manual, top 20 accounts):**
- Research: LinkedIn, news, job postings
- Reference specific detail in Email 1

**Tier 2 (semi-automated, next 100):**
- Use company industry + size to pick right template
- Fill {{pain_point}} based on their industry

**Tier 3 (automated, rest):**
- Let run-campaign.py handle personalisation
- Monitor reply rate and optimise
