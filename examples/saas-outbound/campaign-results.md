# Case Study: SaaS Company Outbound Campaign

**Industry:** B2B SaaS (HR Tech)
**Target:** VP Engineering, Head of IT, CTO at SaaS companies (50-500 employees)
**Location:** Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad
**Tool:** agent-sales-kit + Apollo.io

---

## Campaign Setup

```json
{
  "industry": "SaaS software",
  "location": ["Bengaluru", "Pune", "Hyderabad"],
  "company_size": "51-500",
  "titles": ["VP Engineering", "Head of IT", "CTO", "Director of Engineering"],
  "sequence": "saas-company (5-touch)",
  "duration": "45 days"
}
```

## What Worked
- Subject line "{{company}}'s office network — quick question" had highest open rate (68%)
- Email 3 (free audit offer) generated 40% of all replies
- LinkedIn connection before email increased reply rate by 2x
- Personalization with company headcount from Apollo improved CTR significantly

## What Didn't Work
- Generic subjects ("Following up", "Quick question") had <20% open rate
- Sending on Mondays had lowest reply rate
- InMails without a specific hook got ignored

## Learnings
1. SaaS companies respond best to reliability/uptime pain points, not cost
2. Reference their tech stack or office location for personalization
3. Free audit offer converts better than demo request
4. Best send time: Tuesday/Wednesday 10-11am IST
