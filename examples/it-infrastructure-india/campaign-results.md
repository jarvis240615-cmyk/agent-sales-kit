## What Worked

1. **Hyper-specific subject lines** — "Pune school network upgrade" outperformed generic by 3x
2. **Email 3 (free audit offer)** — highest reply rate of all 5 touches (4.2%)
3. **LinkedIn warm-up before cold email** — 40% higher acceptance when we'd engaged with their content first
4. **Following up after government tender announcements** — 5 replies came from "saw the ZP Pune ICT tender — relevant for you?" subject

## What Didn't Work

- Generic subject lines ("Quick question") — low open rate
- Emails over 100 words — reply rate dropped significantly
- Sending on Mondays — Friday/Saturday worked better for India
