## Email 1 — Day 1
**Subject:** `[Specific observation about {{company}}]`
```
Hi {{first_name}},

[One sentence showing you know their situation.]

Most [their role]s we speak to struggle with [pain point]. We help [target companies] [solution] — usually in [timeframe].

Worth a quick call?

{{sender_name}}
```
