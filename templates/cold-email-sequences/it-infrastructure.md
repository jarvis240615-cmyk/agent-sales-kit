# Cold Email Sequence — IT Infrastructure

Target: IT Managers, CTOs, Purchase Heads at enterprise companies

---

## Email 1 — Day 1 (Problem First)

**Subject:** `{{company}} network infrastructure — quick question`

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Noticed {{company}} has been growing. As teams scale, most IT managers hit the same wall: cabling that wasn't built to handle it, Wi-Fi dead zones, or SD-WAN underdelivering.

We help enterprise IT teams solve this — without ripping and replacing everything.

Worth a 15-min call this week?

{{sender_name}}
{{sender_company}}
```

---

## Email 2 — Day 4 (Social Proof)

**Subject:** `How [Similar Company] cut network downtime by 60%`

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Quick follow-up — we recently helped a mid-size IT company cut network downtime by 60% with a structured cabling + SD-WAN upgrade. Done in 6 weeks, zero business disruption.

Is {{company}} facing similar challenges? Happy to share the case study.

{{sender_name}}
```

---

## Email 3 — Day 10 (Free Value)

**Subject:** `Free IT infrastructure audit for {{company}}`

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Offering a complimentary 2-hour IT infrastructure audit for {{company}} — we map your current setup, identify gaps, give you a prioritised action plan.

No pitch. Just a useful deliverable.

Interested?

{{sender_name}}
```

---

## Email 4 — Day 20 (Direct)

**Subject:** `Last note — {{company}} infrastructure`

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Last email from my end. If you're not the right person or the timing's off, completely understand.

If infrastructure ever becomes a priority: {{sender_phone}} | {{sender_email}}

{{sender_name}}
```

---

## Email 5 — Day 45 (Re-engage)

**Subject:** `Things have changed at {{sender_company}}`

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Reaching back — we've recently [new case study / certification / service expansion]. Thought it might be more relevant for {{company}} now.

Quick catch-up?

{{sender_name}}
```

---

## Tips
- Send emails 9:30–11am or 4–6pm IST for best open rates
- Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday perform best
- Personalise Email 1 with a specific observation about their company
- Don't attach anything in Email 1 — it triggers spam filters
